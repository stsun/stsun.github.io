About Me

I am an oceanographer at the Laoshan Laboratory in Qingdao, China. Prior to this appointment, I was a postdoc at the California Institute of Technology and an Assistant Professor at Florida State University. I obtained my PhD from the Scripps Institution of Oceanography in 2019.

Research in my group advances fundamental understanding of climate dynamics, with a particular emphasis on the physical mechanisms governing interactions among the ocean, atmosphere, cryosphere, and marine ecosystems.
